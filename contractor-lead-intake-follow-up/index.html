Lead intake and follow-up

Turn contractor leads into an organized follow-up workflow.

Missed calls, scattered emails, delayed replies, and incomplete job details cost contractors real opportunities. Sovun Assistant is designed to help capture the lead, organize the facts, and prepare the next step for approval.

A practical lead intake workflow

A good contractor lead workflow should be simple: notice the incoming request, extract the important details, ask for missing information, and keep follow-up from falling through the cracks.

Sovun Assistant can help create that workflow around the company’s real intake sources and preferred communication style.

  • Lead source and contact information
  • Job type, location, timeline, and urgency
  • Known scope details and missing questions
  • Suggested response draft
  • Follow-up reminder or next action

Drafts before sending

For customer communication, the safest model is draft-first. Sovun Assistant prepares the reply in the company voice, but the owner or approved team member reviews before anything customer-facing is sent.

That approach keeps speed and quality together: faster responses, cleaner notes, and no AI making commitments without approval.

  • No automatic pricing commitments
  • No confirmed scheduling without approval
  • No customer-facing send without review
  • Clear separation between known facts and assumptions

From intake to estimate

Once the lead is captured, the assistant can help prepare estimate notes, open questions, site-visit needs, and proposal draft structure. The company still controls final pricing, terms, and whether to accept the job.
